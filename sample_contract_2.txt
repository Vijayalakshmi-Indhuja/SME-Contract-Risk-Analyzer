CONSULTING SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Consulting Services Agreement ("Agreement") is made on 01 February 2026 between BrightSoft Solutions Pvt Ltd ("Consultant") and GreenMart Stores ("Company").

1. Services
The Consultant shall provide IT consulting and system support services to the Company.

2. Fees and Payment
The Company agrees to pay the Consultant INR 180,000 within 15 days from the invoice date.

Failure to make payment on time shall result in a penalty of 1.5% per month.

3. Termination
The Company may terminate this Agreement at any time without assigning any reason.

The Consultant must provide at least 60 days prior notice for termination.

4. Liability
The Consultant shall be fully liable for any damages arising out of the services.

5. Indemnity
The Consultant agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the Company from all claims.

6. Confidentiality
Both parties shall keep all business information strictly confidential.

7. Non-Compete
The Consultant shall not engage in competing business for a period of two years.

8. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.

9. Jurisdiction
Courts at Bengaluru shall have exclusive jurisdiction.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, both parties have executed this Agreement.
